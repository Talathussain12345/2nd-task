Halwa Puri Aur Bhindhi ka Salan

Recipe by Talat Hussain

Ingredients

puri

  • Flour 2 cups (Make about 20)
  • Oil 2 tsp
  • Oil for deep frying
    •

Bhindi ka Salan

  • Meadin size leady fingure 1kg
  • Salt to test
  • Zeera powder 1/2 tsp
  • Crushed whole dried red chilies 1/2 tsp
  • Haldi powder 1/4 tsp
    •

Halwa

  • Sujji (samolina) 1 cup
  • Oil 2 tsp
  • Sugar 1-1/2 cup
  • Water 1/2 cup
  • Seeds of small Illaichi 3
  • Yellow food color (optional)

Instruction

Puri

  1. Make the dough with flour and 2 tsp oil and water as needed.
  2. Make into samll puri(pancake like) as flat as you can and deep and deep fry in hot oil.

Bhindi ka Salan

  1. Cut the leady fingure into small cubes.
  2. Add spices and 1 cup of water and leave on low heat.
  3. When soft,mash lightly.

Halwa

  1. put the sujji and oil in a pot along with illaichi seeds and fry until slightly brown on very low heat
  2. Meanwhile combine sugar and water to make a syrup.
  3. Add this to the sujji taking care not to burn yourself.
  4. If you want to color the halwa add the color add the color to the syrup before pouring it into the sujji.
